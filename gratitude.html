Acknowledgements and Gratitude

Poem text by Elizabeth J. Mitchell

Photo by Deniz Fuchidzhiev on Unsplash

The poem form is a choose-your-own-path poem, a maze poem. The form is inspired by Franny Choi's poem "Never Here" from Choi's collection, Floating, Brilliant, Gone. Gratitude to Franny Choi.

Gratitude to 826michigan for allowing me to share the idea of maze poems with young people and to consider the possibilities of how we might share writing digitally.
